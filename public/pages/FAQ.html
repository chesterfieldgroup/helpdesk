Frequently Asked Questions

To submit a support request, navigate to the home page and fill out the "IT Support Request Form." Be sure to include a detailed description of your issue and attach any relevant screenshots.
Your support request should include a brief summary of the issue, a detailed description of the problem, and any screenshots or error messages that might help our team troubleshoot the issue.
Resolution times depend on the complexity of the issue. Simple issues might be resolved within a few hours, while more complex problems could take longer. You can track the status of your support request via email updates.
